Welcome to RegalDanes.com.  All of our Great Dane breeding dogs are family pets that live inside with us here on our 8 acre homestead we affectionately call Regal Plain in Greenfield, Ohio.  We are a small hobby breeder that strives to provide healthy puppies to good homes, our puppies are well socialized by our large family and are used to interacting with adults and small children.

If you think one of our puppies might be right for you and would like to get on our puppy waiting list or just have some questions, please send a message to morgan@regaldanes.com or use the email button on the bottom left of this page.
